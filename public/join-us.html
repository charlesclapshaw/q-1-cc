GAllery MANAGER - starting may 2023
As we build a studio of artisans with limitless technology, we are looking for a Gallery Manager to assist with the sales and management of our gallery, to be launched in late April 2023.

As the Gallery Manager at Futures of Art, you are a vital member of the team and its growth, starting with our gallery in Bondi as well as the internets.

The focus of the Gallery Manager  role will be:
  • Manage the day by day running of the gallery, organise studio visits and open gallery days 3 x per week
  • Help promote the gallery to the local community and participate in local community events
  • Manage and grow the ongoing relationship with existing and new galleries and museums
  • Collaborate with founders on the marketing plan and content calendar for Futures of Art
  • Deliver and report metrics of the success of sales and marketing initiatives
  • Work with artists, creators and innovators to change the current reality
