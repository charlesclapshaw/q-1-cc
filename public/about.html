WHo ARE FUTURES OF ART?

we are ARTISTS WHO EXPLORE OUTCOMES WITH FUTURE TECHNOLOGY(S)
With a studio of artisans and limitless technology, we are creators and thinkers, questioning our current realities and plans for the near future.

Fortunate to have the resources necessary to create and build, we prototype and question value propositions in technology with art.

We don't dream the futures, we actively work within them - testing new technologies in public settings with international galleries, artists and institutions.
